Congestus - Capture The Flag
(The Nest)

Objective: Get to the enemies flag room
and still their flag via three different
routes: Airlift, ladder, or battlements.
Bring it back to your capture point balcony
to score 10 points for your team.

Scoring: 10 points per Capture.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
