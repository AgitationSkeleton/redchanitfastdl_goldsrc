Biohazard - Capture The Flag
Original map from Jabroni Brawl: Episode 3 by Nask and Zero.

================  Objective  ================
Enter the enemy Pump Station and make your way 
to where their Flag resides in the Flag Room. 
Grab it and bring it back to your Flag Room. 
Place it on the raised square to Capture it. 

================  Scoring  ================
10 points per Capture.

================  Notes  ================
- Flag carriers drop the Flag when they die. 
- Dropped flags return to their Base after 60 seconds.
- Resupply lockers in respawn rooms replenish health, 
armor and ammo that your class use.
