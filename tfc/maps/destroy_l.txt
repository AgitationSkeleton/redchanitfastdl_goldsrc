Destroy_l - 6v6 (Works 8v8 also)
Made by Koochy

Objective: Storm the enemy Base and make
your way to where their Flag resides at the 
rear of the base. Grab it and bring it back 
to your Base. Place the flag on the platform 
located opposite your security control.

Scoring: 10 points per Capture.

Security: Resets after 30 seconds.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 45 seconds.
