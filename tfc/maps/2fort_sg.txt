2Fort_SG (9th April 1999)

Objective:
Grab the opponent's Flag from their
Base,bring it back to your Base and 
"Capture it" by running over the 
pedestal in the middle of your 
Balcony outside.
The Flag is dropped when the carrier
dies,and remains on that spot for 60
seconds before returning home.

Scoring:
You score 10 points for each member
of your team per Capture.

If you find you cannot get on the lift
in the blue base,try approaching from
the right hand side.
