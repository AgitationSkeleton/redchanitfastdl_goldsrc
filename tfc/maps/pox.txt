Monkeypox - CTF Infiltration

Objective: The Red and Blue teams are 
trying to poison each other with a Bioweapon. 
The Bioweapon starts in the freezer on the 
boat. They have to carry the leaky 
Bioweapon to the ventilation room in the 
enemy base. 

The Yellow team must protect the Bioweapon 
by keeping it in the Freezer at all times. 

Scoring: 25 points per delivery.  2 points
to Yellow every 15 seconds if the Bioweapon
is in the Freezer.
