The Signal - Capture / Defend

Objective: The Attacking Team starts on the
top of a war torn building. With their Flag,
they must capture a communications Truck
that is in a garage at the base of this building.
The Defending Team has to defend the
Truck for 10 minutes. Teams swap roles in
the next round.

Scoring: 25 points to the Team that wins the 
round. 3 points for every kill of a flagholder.

Note: You cannot drop the flag once it is
picked up. Red defends first.
