Most - Capture The Flag

Objective:
Enter the enemy Base and make
your way to where their Flag resides in the
Basement. To capture it, bring it to your team's truck.

Scoring: 10 points per Capture.

Other Notes:Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds.
Most buttons are touch and shoot activated.
