Rasen by Hoedog

Objective: Blue team must secure 2 capture
points, and then finally secure Red's HQ.

Red team must prevent the capturing of
their HQ before the 25+ minute time limit
runs out.

Scoring: Red team  - 1 points per 10 seconds
	 Blue team - 50 points for cp 1
	           - 50 points for cp 2
		   - 60 points for HQ

Other Notes: When a capture point is secured, 
spawn points are revised and a new flag 
appears. Rounds last 25+ minutes.
