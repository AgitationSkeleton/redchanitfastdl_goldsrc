Eleventh Hour - Tug of War

Take 2 keycards to both of your team's 
security doors to gain access to the 
Control Center. Once you are in the 
Control Center use 2 keycards to launch 
the missile.

Scoring: 25 points per launch. 5 points
for every security door opened.

Other Notes: There are Blue, Red and Green
keycards.
