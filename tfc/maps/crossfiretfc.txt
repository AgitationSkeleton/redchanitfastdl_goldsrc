CrossfireTFC by Lee W
Based on the original crossfire
4 team Nuke the enemy
recomended players 8-24

Grab your key when the doors open and take
it to the bunker to call the nuke.  

Scoring: 10 team points and 10 frags for calling the nuke,
10 team points for any team which has one or 
more players inside the bunker when the nuke hits.
5 frags for being inside the bunker when nuke hits.

Note you carn't build directly infront of the
resupply room doors and you carn't spam them.
If you follow an enemy into their respawn 
then you will get killed by the laser.
